Markus Blessing

Nature and Landscape Photographer

From Maple Ridge, BC

Welcome to my photography portfolio! I am Markus Blessing, a nature and landscape photographer based in Maple Ridge, British Columbia.

I have a deep love and appreciation for the beauty of the natural world, and I strive to capture this beauty through my photography.

Whether I am out exploring the forests and mountains of BC, or wandering along the shores of the Pacific Ocean.

My goal is to create images that evoke a sense of awe and wonder in those who view them.
